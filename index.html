The Journey to Mastery

Phase 1: Explore & Learn

Start with free resources. Watch YouTube tutorials, read docs, and familiarize yourself with the basic tools of your chosen field.

Phase 2: Build & Create

Move away from tutorials. Build your first website, edit a short film, or produce a 1-minute track. Hands-on practice is key.

Phase 3: Portfolio & Network

Compile your best work. Create a Github, Behance, or YouTube channel. Connect with like-minded creators online.

Phase 4: Launch Career

Start freelancing, apply for internships, or launch your own indie project/startup. Monetize your passion.
